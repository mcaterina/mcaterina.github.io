Why I like This Movie

Little Big Soldier is my favorite movie for many reasons, first is the historical backround in the movie and having it take place in such an interesting area and time period. I also enjoy the scenery and story telling throughout the movie, but my favorite element of the movie is the Lesson. In the movie the two main characters learn the opposite lesson that some things are worth fighting for and some things aren't worth fighting for. The ending of the movie was perfect, and I may or may not of cried at the end of the movie.
