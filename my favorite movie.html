My Favorite Movie

Little big soldier is my favorite movie for many reasons, first is the historical bakround in the movie and having it take place in such an intresting area and timeperiod. I also enjoy the scenary and story telling throughout the movie, but my favorite element of the movie is the moral. In the movie the two main characters learn the opposite lesson that somethings are worth fighting for and somthings aren't worth fighting for. The ending of the movie was perfect, and I may or maynot of cried at the end of the movie.
